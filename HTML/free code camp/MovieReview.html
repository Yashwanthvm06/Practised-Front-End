Movie Review: Coolie

Coolie is an upcoming Indian Tamil-language action thriller film directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj and produced by Kalanithi Maran under Sun Pictures. Delves into a man's relentless quest for vengeance since youth, driven by righting past wrongs, shaping his very existence. Viewers experience the complexities of his tumultuous vendetta journey.

Movie Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (9.2/10)

Cast Members

  • Rajinikanth as Deva
  • Aamir Khan as Dahaa
  • Nagarjuna as Simon
  • Pooja Hegde as Monica
